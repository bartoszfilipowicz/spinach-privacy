Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find restaurants near me?

Open the app and allow location access when prompted. The map will automatically show vegan and vegetarian-friendly restaurants nearby. You can also search for a specific city or area using the search bar.

How do I save a restaurant to my favorites?

Tap on any restaurant to view its details, then tap the heart icon to add it to your favorites. You can access your saved places from the Saved tab.

The app shows no restaurants in my area

We're constantly expanding our coverage. If you don't see restaurants in your area, try zooming out or searching for a nearby larger city. You can also email us to request coverage for your area.

How do I report incorrect information?

If you notice incorrect information about a restaurant (hours, address, vegan options, etc.), please email us at spinach.guide@gmail.com with the restaurant name and the correction needed.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including favorites and preferences. Alternatively, email us at spinach.guide@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Spinach caches restaurant data for areas you've visited, so you can browse previously loaded restaurants without an internet connection. However, you'll need internet access to load new areas or see the latest updates.

How is the vegan-friendliness rating calculated?

Our rating system considers multiple factors including the variety of vegan options, plant-based protein availability, menu labeling, and staff knowledge. Restaurants are rated on a scale that helps you quickly identify the best options for your dietary preferences.
